New User Registration

The steps for the Registration of New User is Mentioned Below :-


  1. Click on the New User Tab which is available on the screen.
  2. Enter your mobile no. and email id along with other details.
  3. Select the terminals from terminal list.
  4. Upload supporting documents under tab (Single pdf with max. 1 mb siz.
  5. Click on submit Button.
  6. Enter OTP sent to your registered mobile.
  7. You will get Registration details saved successfully.
